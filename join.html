How to join

When the web was new, design and structure were all mixed up together. Eventually we realised this was very messy, so we invented CSS and separated design from HTML. Everyone felt much better after this, and went and had a cup of tea.

  1. Go to the digital screen in the Living Room at Zoku.
  2. Tap on the workshop you like the most and click to join.
  3. Last but not least, think about your talents. Maybe someday others can join you in a workshop you create yourself.
